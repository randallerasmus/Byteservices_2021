Company History

Welcome to ashCartridges, your number one source for all things cartridges and printers. I am dedicated to providing you the very best of printers,cartridges and ink, with an emphasis on quality,quick response time and effortless supply.

Founded in 2010 by Ashley Smith, ashCartridges has come a long way from its beginnings in Eersteriver. When Ashley first started out, his passion for cartridges, printers drove him to start his own business.

I hope you enjoy our products as much as I enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
